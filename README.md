## What it does

- **Listens to the preacher** through your sound desk or microphone.
- **Detects Bible references** ("Romans 12:1", "let's turn to Psalm 23", "John chapter 3 verse 16") in real time.
- **Pushes the verse to your livestream output** — OBS, vMix, ProPresenter, or directly to a second monitor.
- **Voice commands** for the operator: *"change to NIV"*, *"delete previous verse"*, *"show verse 7"*.
- **Works in 17 translations**: NIV, NLT, ESV, MSG, AMP, KJV, NKJV, NASB, CSB, NRSV, RSV, ASV, NCV, GNT, CEV, ERV, WEB.

## Quick start

1. **Download** the installer from [is.gd/scriptureliveai](https://is.gd/scriptureliveai).
2. **Run `Setup.exe`.** Windows may show a blue "Windows protected your PC" warning — click **More info → Run anyway**. (The installer is unsigned for now; the warning is normal.)
3. **Open ScriptureLive AI.** First launch shows a welcome screen explaining the **3-hour free trial**.
4. **Pick your audio source** (sound desk feed or microphone) on the home screen.
5. **Add the verse window** to your livestream software:
   - **OBS / vMix:** add a "Window Capture" source pointing at the *ScriptureLive Output* window.
   - **Single monitor setup:** drag the output window onto your secondary display.
6. Start preaching — verses appear automatically.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| "Windows protected your PC" on install | Click **More info → Run anyway**. The installer is unsigned. |
| Verses not appearing | Check the audio source meter on the home screen — if it's not moving, the wrong device is selected. |
| Wrong translation showing | Say *"change to NIV"* (or NLT/ESV/etc.) into the mic, or change it in **Settings → Bible**. |
| App says "Trial expired" | Get an activation key from your seller and enter it in **Admin → License**. |
| Need to start over | Uninstall via Windows **Add/Remove Programs**, then reinstall. License will need to be re-entered. |
